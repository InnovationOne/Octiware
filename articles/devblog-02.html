DEV-Blog #2 – New Features Unveiled!

Published on January 31, 2025

Greetings, Esteemed Travelers of Renalvia!

Once again, we open the doors to our humble workshop, where gears turn, steam rises, and ideas take form. This month, we've made considerable strides in refining our intricate mechanisms—from enhanced lighting contraptions to a sophisticated combat engine. Prepare yourselves for an inside look at our recent inventions!

Combat System

This month's grand endeavor was refining the art of battle—perfecting weapon interactions using precision-engineered Collider2D mechanisms that ensure every strike feels authentic and rewarding. Our automaton adversaries now boast sophisticated behaviors, shifting seamlessly between idle pondering, vigilant searching, and calculated attacks.

Highlights

  • Diverse Foes: Confront cunning melee brutes, elusive ranged marksmen, and enigmatic spellcasters.
  • Weaponry of Distinction: Arm yourself with finely-crafted daggers, ornate flails, swords, bows, and more, each potentially imbued with elemental power—fire, frost, or electricity.
  • Precision Mechanics: A state-of-the-art hitbox system precisely registers strikes, immersing you deeper into the thrill of battle.
  • Treasure & Spoils: Each enemy yields valuable rewards to the fortunate and daring victor.

Character Customization

Our innovative Tailor Engine is complete! Now adventurers can meticulously alter their appearance and enhance their attributes with a rich assortment of attire—from sturdy gloves to polished boots, dashing hats, and resilient armor.

Highlights

  • Extensive Wardrobe: Gloves, helmets, belts, body armor, trousers, and footwear; each with unique abilities.
  • Personalized Appearances: Select your persona—from rugged adventurers to elegant inventors, with diverse builds, ages, and tones befitting Renalvia’s diverse inhabitants.
  • Seamless Attire Management: A sophisticated, user-friendly inventory interface ensures instant, intuitive outfit swaps through simple drag-and-drop actions.
  • Animation Generator: Seamlessly crafts animations for diverse clothing styles and weaponry, ensuring fluid and visually captivating character movement.

Behold the inner workings of our Tailor Engine interface: On the left, witness your character adorned with gear forged in our creative foundry; on the right, a treasure trove of customizable attire awaits your command. A simple drag-and-drop transforms your avatar like clockwork.

In this scene, marvel at the dynamic interplay of design and function: our innovative system brings each garment to life, making every piece a vital cog in your steampunk adventure.

Prefab Spawner and Dynamic World Design

Our patented Prefab Spawner breathes life into Renalvia's landscapes, dispersing resources like sturdy oaks, venerable rocks, and fallen timber naturally across the terrain. Players will gather materials while exploring forests, beaches, or subterranean mines, each location offering endless opportunity and surprises.

Demonstration:

The image displays a collection of trees and rocks on a farmland area, representing a vibrant environment. Players can use these resources to gather materials and explore the world further. This system is also utilized in areas like beaches and mines.

Map Concept Design

This month, our mapmakers completed preliminary designs for the Renalvian territories, featuring five distinct regions: tranquil Beaches, a bustling City Center, fertile Farms, majestic Mountains, and resource-rich Mines. These meticulous layouts serve as a canvas for future adventures, immersive NPC interactions, and environmental storytelling.

Lighting Enhancements

The chronometer now dictates not only the hour but breathes life into our world. Witness as sunlight elegantly glides across Renalvia, casting lengthening shadows and bathing landscapes in warm, natural hues as day transitions seamlessly into night—thanks to the carefully tuned TimeManager mechanism.

Clock (UI)

Introducing our first completed Chronometer Interface; a beautifully designed clock that combines the elegance of analog timekeeping with precise digital readouts.

Each season reveals its distinct essence through animated details: from delicate spring petals and lush summer leaves to autumn’s gentle fall and winter’s drifting snowflakes.

Also, enjoy weather updates and forecasts displayed elegantly alongside the clock face.
