DEV-Blog #1 – The Adventure Begins!

Published on January 10, 2025

Greetings, Esteemed Adventurers and Explorers!

Step aboard and welcome to the maiden voyage of our DEV-Blog—a chronicle of invention, mystery, and boundless curiosity. After nearly three years of passionate tinkering behind closed doors and six months of exhilarating teamwork, we open our workshop doors to share our journey through the wondrous lands of Renalvia. Gear up and prepare to delve into the hidden mechanisms and intricate lore of our upcoming steampunk adventure.

In our game, you assume the role of a leader summoned to the capital Norinwall

In this game, you will don the mantle of a visionary leader, summoned from afar to the bustling capital of Norinwall. Tasked by the renowned strategist Garreth Larsen himself, you'll breathe life back into a forgotten outpost on a mysterious island—an island steeped in ancient secrets, steam-powered wonders, and echoes of a tragic past. Your choices will forge not only your destiny but the very history of Renalvia.

Platforms: The game will initially launch on PC (Steam, Epic, GoG) and, hopefully, later on Xbox, PlayStation, Switch, and even on smartphones (iOS, Android).

Target Audience: Our game is primarily designed for steampunk enthusiasts and casual gamers.

What We’re Proud Of So Far

Even though our mechanical wonder remains under diligent construction, we’ve already brought to life astonishing inventions:

  • A dynamic weather contraption—experience rainstorms, snowfalls, and tempestuous skies.
  • A charmingly detailed agricultural mechanism, enabling cultivation from seed to harvest.
  • An intricate system of economics and quests, rich with steampunk flavor.
  • A robust inventory and interactive dialogue engine.
  • Intriguing crafting and cooking mechanics, and detailed resource management.

Our workshop employs tools such as Unity, C#, FMOD, and Aseprite. Despite humble beginnings and prototypes still evolving, each cog and gear brings us immense pride.

Music and Sound

Music is the soul of our steampunk tale. Beyond an evocative main theme, our soundscapes ebb and flow through the seasons: from breezy spring melodies to winter’s contemplative whispers. Listen closely—each track tells a tale, shifting gracefully from moments of introspection to crescendos of heroism.
