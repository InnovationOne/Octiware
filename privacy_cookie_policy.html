Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 10, 2025

Welcome to Octiware. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal data (if any) when you visit our website or interact with us.

1. Information We Collect

Currently, we do not collect any personal data from our users. Our website is hosted by Cloudflare and GitHub Pages, which may gather technical data such as IP addresses, browser types, and access times in their server logs. This data is used solely to ensure the security and performance of our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect personal data, we do not use any personal information for marketing or service improvements. The technical data collected by Cloudflare and GitHub Pages is used exclusively to optimize website performance and security.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not share any personal data with third parties. However, if required by law, we reserve the right to disclose technical data to authorities or other authorized entities.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to secure your data. Although we do not store personal data, we protect technical data from unauthorized access and misuse.

5. Data Retention

Technical data collected by our hosting services (Cloudflare and GitHub Pages) is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above or as required by law.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not process personal data, you do not have specific rights regarding access, correction, or deletion of your personal information. If you have any questions or concerns about our data practices, please contact us using the information provided below.

7. International Data Transfers

Our hosting services (Cloudflare and GitHub Pages) may transfer data to countries outside your residence. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and that appropriate security measures are in place.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our data practices.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at:

Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 10, 2025

Our website does not use proprietary cookies. However, third-party services such as Cloudflare may set technical cookies to ensure the security and performance of our website. These cookies are strictly necessary for the website’s operation and do not require your consent.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your computer or device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for the functioning of the website, enabling basic features such as page navigation and access to secure areas. Our website exclusively uses these necessary cookies provided by our hosting services (Cloudflare).

3. Managing Cookies

Since we do not set any non-essential cookies, there is no need for you to take any action. The strictly necessary cookies set by third parties are vital for the website’s functionality and cannot be disabled without affecting performance.

4. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Please review this policy periodically to stay informed about our use of cookies.
