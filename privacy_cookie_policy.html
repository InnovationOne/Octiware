Privacy & Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 07, 2025

Welcome to Octiware! Your privacy matters deeply to us. Currently, we do not collect any personal information about our visitors; just technical data necessary to operate the site efficiently and safely.

About Your Privacy

We respect your privacy deeply. At present, Octiware does not collect, store, or process any personal data from you. The only information collected is basic technical data (like IP addresses and device type), which our trusted partners Cloudflare and GitHub Pages use solely for security, content delivery, and optimal performance of our website.

Use of Cookies

Our website uses cookies solely to ensure that everything works smoothly. Cookies are small data files stored on your device, helping us optimize loading times, maintain security, and provide you with the best browsing experience possible. Currently, we only use cookies provided by Cloudflare and GitHub Pages for performance and security purposes; never to track your personal activity or identity.

Types of Cookies We Use

We currently use the following types of cookies:

  • Technical cookies: Essential cookies for site functionality and security.
  • Performance cookies: Cookies from Cloudflare that optimize site performance and provide security enhancements.

Third-Party Services

We utilize trusted third-party services, including Cloudflare and GitHub Pages, to ensure security, reliability, and optimal site performance. These services may collect limited technical data, such as your IP address, purely for performance and security reasons. For detailed information, please review their privacy policies directly.

Your Rights

While we currently do not store personal data, your trust is important to us. If you ever have questions, concerns, or simply want to reach out, we encourage you to contact us anytime at info@octiware.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy & Cookie Policy to reflect technical changes or legal updates. Any changes will be clearly communicated here, so feel free to revisit regularly.

Contact Us

Questions or concerns about our privacy practices? We're always here for you. Contact us at info@octiware.com.
