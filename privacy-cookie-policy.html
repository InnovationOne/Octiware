Privacy & Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2025

We respect your privacy. This policy tells you what we collect, how we use it, and what you can do about it.

1. Who we are

Octiware is an independent game studio based in Nürnberg, Germany. When we say "we", "us", or "our", we mean the Octiware team building worlds out of pixels and caffeine.

2. What data we collect

We keep things minimal. When you visit our site, we may collect:

  • Emails you send us: When you contact us, apply for jobs, or sign up for something. Voluntarily, of course.

3. What we do with your data

We use your data to:

  • Respond to your messages or job applications.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties unless required by law.

4. Cookies & tracking

We hate cookie banners too. That’s why we only use cookies when we absolutely must:

  • Essential cookies: For saving language preferences or style/theme selections.

You can clear or block cookies in your browser settings anytime.
We recommend using Firefox. It respects your privacy and doesn't treat your data like currency.

5. Your rights (GDPR & beyond)

If you're in the EU (or just privacy-conscious), you have the right to:

  • Access the data we have about you
  • Correct or delete it
  • Limit or object to how we use it
  • File a complaint with a data protection authority

To do any of this, just email us: info@octiware.com

6. Data storage & security

We store data securely on servers located in the EU. We use encryption, strong passwords, and good coffee-fueled judgment to keep it safe.

7. Updates to this policy

We may tweak this page now and then. Big changes? We’ll let you know. Tiny legal wordings? Probably not.

8. Questions?

If anything’s unclear or you’re worried about data stuff, just reach out: info@octiware.com
