2D Top-Down Pixel Artist

Location: Remote/Worldwide

Project: Steamy Isle

Share this offer:

About Us

We are an international team of developers, designers, and storytellers creating a unique, narrative-driven gaming experience. Steamy Isle combines classic pixel art aesthetics with an innovative steampunk twist. If you value creative freedom, open collaboration, and the chance to contribute to an ambitious project, you’ve come to the right place.

About the Project

Steamy Isle is a 2D top-down pixel art RPG set in a vibrant steampunk world. Blending exploration, adventure, and strategy, our game is inspired by titles such as Haiku the Robot, A Short Hike, and Moonlighter to deliver an atmospheric and engaging experience.

  • Platforms: PC, Consoles (Xbox, PlayStation, Switch), Mobile
  • Target Audience: Steampunk enthusiasts and casual gamers worldwide
  • Current Status: Prototype phase with a planned beta in 2025

Your Tasks

As a 2D Pixel Artist, you will play a key role in defining the visual identity of Steamy Isle. Your responsibilities include:

  • Graphic Creation: Crafting character sprites, environments, and objects in a pixel art style.
  • Animation: Developing smooth and engaging animations, including idle, movement, and interactive sequences.
  • Visual Consistency: Establishing and maintaining a unified visual style inspired by our steampunk setting.
  • Collaboration: Coordinating closely with the UI team, concept artists, and developers.
  • Feedback Implementation: Adjusting assets based on internal and external feedback.
