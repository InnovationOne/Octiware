About Us

At Octiware, we bring together developers, artists, designers, and storytellers from around the globe. We’re united by a shared passion for creating captivating, narrative-driven games. Combining classic pixel art with a fresh steampunk aesthetic, we’re crafting an immersive RPG adventure. If you cherish creative freedom, teamwork, and the opportunity to shape something remarkable, we can’t wait to meet you.

About the Project

Our project is an atmospheric 2D top-down pixel art RPG set in a vibrant steampunk universe. Inspired by charming indie titles like Haiku the Robot, A Short Hike, and Moonlighter, we blend exploration, strategic gameplay, and storytelling to immerse players in a unique experience.

  • Platforms: PC (Steam, Epic, GoG), consoles (Xbox, PlayStation, Switch), and mobile (iOS, Android).
  • Target Audience: Designed for steampunk enthusiasts and casual gamers worldwide
  • Current Status: Currently in prototype; beta launch targeted for 2026.

Your Tasks

As a 2D Pixel Artist at Octiware, you'll breathe life into our steampunk world. You’ll create captivating character sprites, immersive environments, and beautifully animated assets. Every pixel matters – your artwork will shape our players’ adventures.

  • Graphic Creation: Bring characters, objects, and environments to life through vibrant pixel art.
  • Animation: Create dynamic animations (idle, movement, actions) that enrich gameplay.
  • Visual Consistency: Ensure a unified visual style that deeply immerses players in our steampunk universe.
  • Collaboration: Work closely with our UI designers, concept artists, and development team to refine and perfect the game.
  • Feedback Implementation: Adapt your creations thoughtfully based on team and community feedback.

Your Profile

  • Expertise: You have a proven talent in pixel art and animation, ideally experienced with Aseprite or Photoshop. Unity experience is beneficial, but not mandatory.
  • Portfolio: A strong portfolio showcasing your pixel art and animation skills is highly preferred.
  • Experience: Previous game development experience is a plus, but enthusiasm, creativity, and passion are most important.
  • Soft Skills: You're communicative, collaborative, receptive to feedback, and eager to learn.
  • Language Skills: Fluent English (written and spoken).
  • Working Style: Comfortable working remotely with flexible hours.
