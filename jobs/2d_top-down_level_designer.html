About Us

At Octiware, we bring together developers, artists, designers, and storytellers from around the globe. We’re united by a shared passion for creating captivating, narrative-driven games. Combining classic pixel art with a fresh steampunk aesthetic, we’re crafting an immersive RPG adventure. If you cherish creative freedom, teamwork, and the opportunity to shape something remarkable, we can’t wait to meet you.

About the Project

Our project is an atmospheric 2D top-down pixel art RPG set in a vibrant steampunk universe. Inspired by charming indie titles like Haiku the Robot, A Short Hike, and Moonlighter, we blend exploration, strategic gameplay, and storytelling to immerse players in a unique experience.

  • Platforms: PC (Steam, Epic, GoG), consoles (Xbox, PlayStation, Switch), and mobile (iOS, Android).
  • Target Audience: Designed for steampunk enthusiasts and casual gamers worldwide
  • Current Status: Currently in prototype; beta launch targeted for 2026.

Your Tasks

As a Level Designer, your vision will shape the very heart of our game's exploration and narrative.

  • Craft Engaging Levels: Design detailed, immersive maps that enhance player exploration and narrative depth.
  • Environmental Storytelling: Create environments that tell compelling stories without words, deepening player immersion.
  • Collaborate Creatively: Work closely with artists, developers, and storytellers to ensure visual and thematic cohesion.
  • Iterate & Improve: Refine levels based on extensive testing and team feedback, constantly enhancing player experience.
  • Technical Integration: Use level-design tools such as Tiled or Unity effectively to bring your designs into the game.

Your Profile

  • Expertise: You have proven experience in 2D level design, preferably in RPG or adventure games. Knowledge of Unity and tile-based tools (e.g., Tiled) is advantageous.
  • Portfolio: Present examples of your previous level design work, demonstrating creativity and thoughtful design.
  • Creative Freedom: You enjoy maximum creative freedom—as long as your designs align with the game’s story and artistic style.
  • Experience: Previous experience in game development is beneficial, but your creativity and passion are key.
  • Soft Skills: Strong communication, teamwork, openness to feedback, and adaptability.
  • Language Skills: Fluent English (written and spoken).
  • Working Style: Comfortable with remote, flexible working conditions.
