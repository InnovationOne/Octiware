About Us

We are an international team of developers, designers, and storytellers creating a unique, narrative-driven gaming experience. Our game combines classic pixel art aesthetics with an innovative steampunk twist. If you value creative freedom, open collaboration, and the chance to contribute to an ambitious project, you’ve come to the right place.

About the Project

Our game is a 2D top-down pixel art RPG set in a vibrant steampunk world. Blending exploration, adventure, and strategy, our game is inspired by titles such as Haiku the Robot, A Short Hike, and Moonlighter to deliver an atmospheric and engaging experience.

  • Platforms: PC, Consoles (Xbox, PlayStation, Switch), Mobile
  • Target Audience: Steampunk enthusiasts and casual gamers worldwide
  • Current Status: Prototype phase with a planned beta in 2025

Your Tasks

As a 2D Level Designer, you will be responsible for shaping the game's world by designing immersive and logical layouts. Your work will be the foundation for the player's experience of exploration and discovery. Your responsibilities include:

  • Map Layout Design: Create balanced and visually appealing maps, detailing the placement of buildings, pathways, and key landmarks.
  • Environmental Storytelling: Design levels that convey narrative and enhance the player’s understanding of the steampunk world.
  • Collaboration: Work closely with artists, writers, and developers to integrate assets and ensure cohesion between gameplay and visual design.
  • Iteration: Use playtesting feedback to refine and improve level designs.
